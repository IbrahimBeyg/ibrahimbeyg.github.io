How I Built a Client‑Generating Website for a Fitness Coach

Bilal, an IFPA-certified fitness coach in Islamabad, wanted to grow beyond word-of-mouth referrals and turn Facebook ad clicks into paying clients — but had no landing page to send them to.

⚡ The Challenge

Bilal was spending on Facebook ads but without a proper landing page, potential clients slipped away in the feed. He needed a niche-focused fitness website that could convert visitors into real leads.

🎯 The Solution

I worked closely with Bilal to design a conversion-focused site from the ground up. Key steps included:

  • Studied competitor fitness sites to identify what drove conversions — clear CTAs, testimonials, and trust signals.
  • Designed a bold, energetic brand with motivational imagery that reflected Bilal's coaching style.
  • Added a WhatsApp lead-capture button offering a free "7-Day Fitness Plan" to collect contact details.
  • Installed Facebook Pixel to track conversions and optimize ad performance over time.
  • Ensured full mobile responsiveness — critical since most Facebook ad traffic arrives via phone.
